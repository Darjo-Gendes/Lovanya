# Home — the morning ritual

**She opens Lovanya because…** she's deciding what to wear and wants to feel
sure before she leaves.

**Three-second feeling:** personally greeted — the app knows her name, her
morning, her weather. **Primary action:** "Analyze Today's Outfit" (the Daily
Style Check card). Everything else supports it.

**Hero:** the Daily Style Check card (blush gradient, outfit photo bleeding
off-edge with mask fade, one gradient CTA). Nothing else on Home may compete
with it.

**Structure (canonical, from the prototype):** wordmark + icon tiles → Dancing
Script greeting → huge Playfair name → soft sub-line → weather line → Daily
Style Check hero → quiet "dress me from my closet" link → three stat tiles
(streak / looks saved / top style) → Today's Insight card → nav.

**Emotional anchors:** the greeting IS the memory hook — script type, her
name at 42px serif, a line that reads like a friend ("Let's create a
beautiful day, inside and out"). The flower deco placeholder (top-right) is
reserved brand space — soft, never busy.

**Guardrails:** stats are affirmations ("Keep it glowing!"), never analytics.
Insight card speaks in Lovanya's voice about HER ("Confidence is your best
accessory"), not about features. No badges, no notification pressure. If Home
ever needs a second CTA of equal weight, the design is wrong — re-audit.
