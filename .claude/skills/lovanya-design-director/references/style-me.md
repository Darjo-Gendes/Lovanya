# Style Me — the ritual (occasion → camera → analyzing → verdict → keepsake)

**She taps Style Me because…** "I don't know if this outfit works" — she
wants confidence before leaving home.

**Three-second feeling:** taken care of. A stylist's attention, not a
scanner. **Primary action per stage — exactly one:** pick occasion →
Continue · shutter · (wait) · Save to Journal · Share Card.

**Stage notes:**
- **Occasion first** (locked order): serif question ("Where are you going…"),
  free-text note — HER words become the look's title ("Summer Brunch") —
  then six pictorial chips. Skip is always allowed; never interrogate.
- **Camera:** encouragement, not instructions ("Stand back, relax, and let
  natural light do the magic ✦"). Real capture with graceful upload fallback.
- **Analyzing:** anticipation theater with honest pacing (~3s): progress ring,
  checklist ticking ("Understanding your style…"), and the reassurance line —
  "Lovanya looks at styling, coordination, and presentation — not
  perfection." Never a spinner.
- **Results:** the verdict is a compliment first. Hero photo full-bleed,
  eyebrow "Style Reflection", Playfair headline ("Quiet confidence — it's
  all here"), personal aura note, then What Works (sage), then ONE gentle
  thought (gold) — never a list of faults. Score appears as a soft badge
  ("87 · Style Score"), never a grade at the top.
- **Share card:** a keepsake, not an export — snapshot card with strengths
  bars, reflection, "Captured with Lovanya" foot. Buttons: Share Card + Back
  to Home only (leaving without sharing is a first-class path).

**Ribbon on results** = "mark as memorable" (Bold variant when active).
Saving files the garment into her closet through the dedup courtesy —
invisible diligence, zero extra questions.

**Trust rules:** back exists at every stage; analyzing is cancellable;
nothing is posted anywhere without her explicit Share tap; the flow is
immersive (no bottom nav) so nothing competes with the ritual.
